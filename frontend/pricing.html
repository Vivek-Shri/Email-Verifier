FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our email verification service.

How accurate is the email verification? +
Our verification engine achieves 99.9% accuracy through real-time SMTP handshakes, MX record lookups, and advanced heuristics. We don't rely solely on pattern matching - we actually connect to mail servers to confirm mailbox existence.
What is SMTP-level verification? +
SMTP-level verification means we connect directly to the mail server and simulate the email delivery process (without actually sending an email). This allows us to confirm whether the mailbox exists, is active, and can receive messages - providing much higher accuracy than syntax-only checks.
Do you store or keep the emails I verify? +
No. We take privacy seriously. Emails are verified in real-time and are not stored permanently. We only retain anonymized metadata for service improvement and SLA reporting. We are GDPR and CCPA compliant.
Can I integrate this into my existing workflow? +
Absolutely. We provide a comprehensive REST API that can be integrated into any platform. Our API documentation includes code samples in Python, JavaScript, PHP, Ruby, and more. Most integrations take less than 30 minutes to implement.
What happens if I exceed my monthly limit? +
You'll receive a notification when you reach 80% of your limit. If you exceed it, additional verifications are charged at an overage rate ($0.005 per verification for Professional plans). You can also upgrade your plan at any time to avoid overage charges.
