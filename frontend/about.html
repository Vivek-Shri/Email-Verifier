Our Mission

Making email verification accessible to everyone

We believe that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to accurate email verification. Too many companies waste money sending emails to invalid addresses, damaging their sender reputation and hurting their bottom line.

Our mission is to democratize email verification technology that was once only available to enterprise corporations. We've built a platform that's powerful enough for Fortune 500 companies yet affordable for startups and solo developers.

Our Vision

A world where every email that leaves an outbox is delivered to a real, engaged recipient. We're building the infrastructure layer that makes the internet's most important communication channel more reliable and trustworthy.

Our Values

Accuracy over everything - We obsess over verification precision because your sender reputation depends on it.

Transparency - No hidden fees, no dark patterns. Our pricing is straightforward and our methodology is open.

Privacy first - We verify emails without storing them. Your data stays yours.

Performance - Sub-50ms response times because waiting for verification is unacceptable.
